About Us

Dark Arts is a clothing store geared towards the alternative youth of Eau Claire. Our space is designed with a sense of community in mind and our store was created to be a safe space to create and share art, purchase clothing, and make friends! We host shows for local bands to play, provide a studio place for people to create art, and sell clothes that have been distributed by local artists and creators.

Location: Downtown Eau Claire
